# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give actionable feedback on code and ideas.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or block participation for conduct violations.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, open a private security advisory or contact the maintainer through GitHub.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository spaces, issues, pull requests, and related communication channels.
